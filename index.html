Welcome to Tourist Safety System

A comprehensive safety solution for tourists with real-time monitoring and instant emergency response.

Our system integrates cutting-edge technology to ensure your safety during your travels:

  • Blockchain-secured digital identity
  • Real-time location tracking
  • Geofencing alerts for restricted areas
  • Instant emergency response
  • Multilingual support
  • 24/7 assistance

Key Features

Digital ID

Secure blockchain-based digital identity that verifies your tourist status and provides quick access to emergency services.

Real-time Tracking

GPS tracking with geofencing technology to alert you and authorities when entering restricted areas.

Emergency Alerts

One-touch emergency alert system that immediately notifies local authorities with your exact location.

Multilingual Support

Support for over 20 languages to assist tourists from around the world.

Trip Planner

Plan your trips with safety ratings and real-time updates about your destinations.

Mobile App

Emergency Quick Guide
Emergency Steps
  1. Press the emergency button on your app or device
  2. Your location will be shared with authorities
  3. Stay on the line if possible
  4. Move to a safe location if you can
  5. Wait for assistance to arrive
Safety Tips
  • Keep your digital ID updated
  • Share your itinerary with trusted contacts
  • Stay in well-lit, populated areas
  • Avoid sharing travel plans publicly
  • Keep emergency numbers handy
Help Center

To register, go to the Registration section and fill out the form with your personal details, emergency contact information, and travel plans. Your digital ID will be created instantly with blockchain security.

When you press the emergency button, our system immediately notifies the nearest police station, tourism officials, and your emergency contacts. Your precise location is shared, and help is dispatched to your location.

Geofencing creates virtual boundaries around restricted or high-risk areas. If you enter one of these areas, the system will alert you and notify authorities if you don't leave within a specified time.
